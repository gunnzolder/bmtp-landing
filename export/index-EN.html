Body

Work out in a fun way! Healthy bodies mean healthy minds and learning through play is often the most effective way to make kids exercise while helping them to improve their knowledge as well.

Mind

BMTP gives the perfect balance of learning and leisure. Not only it is looks and feels like an engaging game, with colorful illustrations and characters, it's content is developed by the teachers in field, which brings an exceptional value for it as a learning tool.

Team

Team-based game scenarios developed by psychologists boost the kids' ability to cooperate, to spot their own and others strengths and to socialize. The better we act as a team, the easier is our path in the big world of future.
